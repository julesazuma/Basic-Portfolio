About Me


Hello, my name is Jules Azuma. I am a web developer currently in training from the Seattle, Washington area. I am very passionate about web development and design, and I look forward to acquiring new skills so that I can utilize my creativity and build amazing websites and apps that really stand out!

My hobbies include coding practice, sports, working out, video games, cars, and of course last but certainly not least spending time with my family! I have lived in Seattle my whole life, but I have visited many places around the world. I love traveling and experiencing different cultures and I hope to use those experiences to inspire myself to make beautiful web pages!
